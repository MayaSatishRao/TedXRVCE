WHAT IS TEDXRVCE

TedX RVCE is TedX event organized by RVCE, a platform for exceptional ideas, and a catalyst for profound change since 2017.

About TedX

TEDx is a global community that believes passionately in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and ultimately, the world. Our chapter is called TEDxToronto, where x = independently organized. Individual TEDx events like ours are organized by our entirely volunteer-led community, with the goal of sparking deep discussion and intimate connection.
